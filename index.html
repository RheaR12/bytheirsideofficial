About

By Their Side is a community initiative designed to support youth with the tools, language, and confidence to build healthier relationships and prevent intimate partner violence.

We collaborate with schools, faith-based organizations, and community groups to deliver engaging, age-appropriate workshops.

What we believe

Prevention works best when it’s participatory, culturally grounded, and led with care.
